# 004 Capability coverage

PBM must cover the full range of enterprise activity required to run a business.

Coverage is checked against a broad enterprise capability reference list, but PBM does not expose that reference list as product language.

## Coverage rule

Every required business capability must map to:

- A PBM workspace
- A PBM capability
- A PBM activity page or planned activity page
- A business object
- A data object or planned data object
- A coverage status

## Coverage status

Use these statuses:

- Covered
- Partially covered
- Planned
- Needs decision
- Not applicable

## Capability groups

PBM capability coverage is organised by business outcome:

1. Define the business
2. Manage people and authority
3. Manage clients and commercial work
4. Deliver projects and services
5. Buy goods and services
6. Plan operations and resources
7. Control money
8. Manage quality, risk and compliance
9. Manage assets, property and maintenance
10. Report performance and administer the platform

## Product rule

The coverage audit exists to prove completeness.

The workspace model exists to make the product usable.

Do not turn the coverage checklist into the main navigation.
