# Quality, Risk And Compliance Requirements

## Module Scope

- Perspective domains: Compliance, audit, quality, complaints, risk

## Purpose

Embed governance and professional assurance into the full work lifecycle.

## Core Objects

- risk
- control
- compliance_check
- complaint
- audit_event
- review
- action
- document

## Functional Requirements

### QRC-001 Quality Review Workflows

The application shall support peer review, technical review and quality sign-off before issue of controlled outputs.

### QRC-002 Conflicts And Compliance Checks

The application shall support conflicts-of-interest, client onboarding and assignment-related compliance checks.

### QRC-003 Complaints Management

The application shall record complaints, investigation steps, responses, outcomes and retained records.

### QRC-004 Risk Register

The application shall support risks, controls, actions, owners, due dates and residual risk views.

### QRC-005 Audit Trail

The application shall provide evidence-backed audit trails across commercial, operational and document workflows.

### QRC-006 Policy And Evidence Linkage

The application shall link policy records, review actions and evidence to affected business objects and cases.

## Reporting Requirements

- open compliance issues and overdue actions
- complaint ageing and outcomes
- risk heatmaps and control status
- quality review turnaround and rejection causes

## Integration Requirements

- document retention and policy acknowledgement workflows
- optional external incident or risk system integration
